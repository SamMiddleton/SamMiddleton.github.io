Values, Empathy & Self-Awareness in Learning and Programming

Self-awareness as to your limitations and how you learn best is obviously beneficial to learning, helping you know what skills you need to work on and how to do so most effectively.

Empathy is also effective in education because it helps you work together better on group projects and because if you help someone out with something their struggling with, they may be able to help you later when you struggle.

It's important for programmers to have good values and strong ethics because we may end up making systems for handling important data.

Surprises about core learning

I was most surprised by some of the consequences of fixed mindsets that have been shown by studies.

For example: 40% of kids who did well on a challenging test, but were praised for their ability rather than their effort lied about their results.

This surprised me with how easy it was to lead kids into a fixed mindset and how far kids would go in keeping up appearances.
